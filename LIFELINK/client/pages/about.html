About LifeLink

Learn more about our mission to save lives through technology and community engagement.

Our Mission

To ensure no patient dies waiting for blood by creating a smart, real-time blood bank management system that connects donors with those in need.

Our Vision

A world where every patient has immediate access to safe blood, and every donation saves lives through technology-enabled emergency response systems.

Who Can Donate?

Check if you're eligible to donate blood

Eligibility

  • Age between 18-60 years
  • Weight above 50 kg
  • Good general health
  • Hemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL

Temporary Deferral

  • Recent surgery (6 months)
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Recent vaccination (1 month)
  • Minor illness (1 week)

Permanent Deferral

  • HIV/AIDS
  • Hepatitis B/C
  • Heart disease
  • Cancer
